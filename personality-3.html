Product work is what I do. This page is who I am — fashion, food, music, and a few things I would have done with my life if fintech hadn't got there first.

Fun Facts

A few things
to know about me

01

I love fashion. Not in a passive “I like clothes” way — in a “I've been known to plan outfits a week ahead” kind of way. I like the idea of a personal style, and somewhere in a parallel life, I'm a stylist. That's still a sidequest I fully intend to complete.

02

I almost became a DJ. First year of uni, I was deep into music — any genre, anytime, and basically ran the common room DSTV remote. I asked my dad for a DJ controller once and his reply sent me straight back to my books.

03

I take food seriously. Eating out, cooking in, trying recipes I have no business attempting, restaurant-hopping with friends — food is how I explore culture, unwind, and honestly, how I do some of my best thinking. A good meal solves most problems.
