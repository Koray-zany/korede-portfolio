Building Escrow Infrastructure for Nigeria's Social Commerce Market

Social commerce in Nigeria runs on WhatsApp and Instagram, but trust is broken. Buyers fear scams; sellers fear fake transfers. I led product from day one ; conducting discovery with 50+ vendors, designing end-to-end escrow flows covering buyer protection, seller confirmation, and dispute resolution. Built a dual-sided fee model and defined an API-first architecture for marketplace integrations. Secured CBN-licensed fund holding via 9PSB.

Key decisions: tiered pricing (1.5-3% buyer fee, flat seller fee), dispute framework with under 5% manual intervention rate, and API strategy that led directly to Bumpa partnership.

&naira;3.8M GMV in 3 months 40% less abandoned transactions Bumpa partnership secured CBN-licensed via 9PSB

Building a Cross-Border Digital Banking Platform ; MVP to V2

HelloMe Money was created to remove geographical barriers to payments for individuals and SMEs. I led from MVP through V2 ; building the core banking engine, seamless KYC onboarding, multicurrency wallets (GBP→EUR conversion), international transfers, and bill payments across mobile and web. Migrated from third-party processor to in-house double-entry ledger infrastructure.

V2 introduced monetisation (paid currency accounts), FX rate transparency tools, statement generation, referral programme, promo codes, and tuition payment flows for Nigerian students studying abroad.

+202% user retention SEPA + UK Faster Payments FCA / SARB / OFAC compliant In-house ledger migration

Redesigning a Lending Experience to Build Trust and Eliminate Support Dependency

Princeps Credit Systems provided personal loans to salaried Nigerian workers, but the product was broken by distrust. Borrowers relied heavily on marketers and support staff for basic loan actions. I applied direct experience from the marketing and support team, giving me deep context on user behaviour, objections, and trust barriers before writing a single wireframe.

Key decisions: simplified onboarding with upfront eligibility visibility; moved loan top-up, liquidation, and manual repayments into self-service flows; reduced application steps; added full loan status transparency.

15-min loan disbursement Self-service top-up & repayment Reduced support dependency Eligibility shown upfront

Making Blockchain Investing Accessible for Africans

Numeri is a VC firm investing in digital assets and crypto innovations. The challenge: onboard accredited investors into complex blockchain fund structures without friction. I designed the full product ; 4-step onboarding (account creation with accreditation check, fund selection across Blockchain/DeFi/Staking funds, KYC paperwork, application submission), investor dashboard, and crypto payment gateway.

Key insight: accredited investor eligibility verification must happen at step one, not after full onboarding ; preventing wasted time for ineligible users. Product is live and processing real investments.
